Why advertise on YouTube?

YouTube has more than 2 billion monthly active users spending over 20 minutes a day on the platform. Here is why it is the smartest place to put your promotion budget.

Massive exposure

YouTube is the world's second-largest search engine. Detailed targeting means your video reaches the exact audience you want out of billions of daily views.

Influences purchase decisions

Nearly 70% of viewers report buying a product after seeing it on YouTube. Video ads reach consumers at the moment they are most open to discovery.

In-depth analytics

YouTube Studio gives you a gold mine of viewership data. See exactly how audiences discover, watch, and engage with your promoted content.

Increased discovery

Ads can appear in search results, recommendations, and home feeds. These multiple touchpoints make your channel more discoverable across the platform.

Tips on how to promote your YouTube channel

Paid promotion works best on a well-optimized channel. Stack these fundamentals with your campaigns for compounding growth.

Optimize your videos for higher visibility

  • YouTube videos appear in up to 30% of top-10 Google search results, so SEO-optimized videos win twice.
  • Include your target keywords naturally in titles and descriptions.
  • Add relevant tags so YouTube understands exactly who should see your video.

Market your channel, not just your videos

  • Create a channel trailer that instantly communicates who you are and why viewers should subscribe.
  • Organize playlists to maximize session watch time across your library.
  • Share your channel link across every social platform you are active on.

Use YouTube ads to expand your reach

  • YouTube ads put your content in front of massive, precisely targeted audiences fast.
  • Promote your best-performing videos, since content with proven engagement converts new viewers best.
  • Choose from skippable, non-skippable, and in-feed formats depending on your goal.

Stay consistent with content and promotion

  • Repurpose long videos into Shorts to capture scrolling viewers with shrinking attention spans.
  • Publish on a schedule your audience can rely on.
  • Pair consistent uploads with consistent promotion. That combination is what compounds channel growth.

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Everything creators want to know before launching their first campaign.

Are the YouTube views from MerakiMe real or bots?

All views through MerakiMe come from real people on YouTube. We promote your videos as ads directly on the platform, steering clear of bots, click farms, or paid watchers. This means your growth is entirely organic and safe, with no risk to your channel.

Will my results disappear after the campaign?

No, they won't. With MerakiMe, your video is promoted to real people, not bots. Any growth or engagement will be organic and the same as any other viewer.

Do views from MerakiMe count toward my video's watch time?

Views from MerakiMe promotions contribute to your video's overall watch time, which can positively influence your video's visibility in YouTube search results and recommendations.

It's important to understand that while these ad views do contribute to your video's watch time, they do not count towards the 4,000 public watch hours required for channel monetization. This is a consistent policy across all promotions run through the Google/YouTube Ads platform.

However, if a viewer discovers your channel through a MerakiMe ad and proceeds to watch more of your videos organically, the watch time from those subsequent views will count towards your monetization requirements.

How does MerakiMe affect my average watch time and audience retention?

Your video's watch time and audience retention might be similar to or slightly lower than your channel's usual rates when you use MerakiMe. This is normal and expected.

Here's why: your regular viewers, who are already your subscribers, tend to watch your content longer because they like it. When promoting your video with MerakiMe, some viewers who see your ad might not be as interested, which is typical for any advertising.

However, the goal of using MerakiMe is to connect your content with new viewers who are genuinely interested in your niche. Over time, these new viewers may return to watch more of your content, which can positively contribute to your channel's overall performance.

Can I promote monetized videos and earn from them?

Yes! You can promote videos that are monetized. Since MerakiMe runs legitimate YouTube Ads through Google Ads, any ad views on your video are treated the same way as standard YouTube ad impressions for monetization purposes.

How does MerakiMe contribute to long-term channel growth and engagement?

MerakiMe is designed to support sustainable growth for your YouTube channel by connecting your content with genuinely interested viewers through targeted Google Ads campaigns. Here is how MerakiMe supports your channel long-term:

  • Targeted promotion: We use sophisticated targeting to ensure your video ads reach viewers who are most likely to enjoy your content, leading to higher-quality audience discovery.
  • Audience discovery: The core of MerakiMe's service is connecting your content with viewers who have a genuine interest in your niche. These viewers are more likely to return for future content, increasing your channel's visibility on YouTube over time.
  • Campaign insights: Data from your campaign provides valuable feedback on audience preferences, allowing you to refine your content strategy and improve future videos.
  • Full YouTube compliance: All promotions run through Google Ads and adhere to YouTube's terms of service, keeping your channel in good standing and protecting your monetization eligibility.

By prioritizing relevant audience targeting and YouTube compliance, MerakiMe supports meaningful, long-term channel development.

What are earned subscribers, earned likes, and earned views?

When you run a YouTube ad campaign through MerakiMe, Google Ads tracks two types of results:

  • Direct results: Actions that happen during or immediately after someone watches your ad, such as the ad view itself.
  • Earned results: Organic actions that happen after someone discovers your channel through an ad. These include earned subscribers, earned likes, earned views, and earned playlist additions.

For example, a viewer might watch your ad, visit your channel, and then subscribe, like several videos, or add your content to a playlist. These are all "earned" actions because they happened organically as a result of your advertising, not because they were purchased or artificially generated.

Earned metrics are tracked and verified through Google Ads reporting, and you can also confirm them in your YouTube Studio analytics, where all ad-driven activity is labeled as "YouTube advertising."
